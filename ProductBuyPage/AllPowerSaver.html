All Power Saver

Detail Our All Power Saver can save power for all appliances except for purely resistor appliances. It is effective for all other appliances but is most effective for induction appliances like AC, Refrigerator, Washing Machine, Fan, TV, Tube Lights, Water Pumps, Freezer, Mixer Grinders and all types of single phase motors etc. It is also efficient for other electrical appliances to a certain extent. Overall, the longer they are used, better the effect will be.

Technology
All Power Saver uses a state of the art electrical technology to monitor and improve the power factor of your household/office appliances. Additionally, its intelligent technology optimizes the voltage and current demand, thus reducing the active power/KWH demand and achieves 10-30% saving on your electricity bill. It also serve as a voltage stabilizer by storing energy for up to 10 seconds and therefore supplies the load with constant voltage during momentary power surges. This in turn results in longer life span of your electrical appliances.
